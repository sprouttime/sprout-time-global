Graphics Design

Who doesn’t like beautifully designed graphics? Graphic Designers communicate information
visually. They either work freelance or in-house with others in the creative space, designing
beautiful visuals and developing the visual identities of their clients and brands.

Here’s what we’ll cover in the Graphic design track:

  • Introduction to graphic design concepts (color theory, typography, composition, etc.)
  • Hands-on tutorial on graphic design software such as Corel draw, Adobe Photoshop, and Illustrator)
  • Examples of different design projects (logos, brochures, posters, etc.)
  • Tips and tricks for improving design skills.

UI/UX Design

UI/UX Designers (also referred to as Product Designers) have the most significant impact on defining how people interact with experiences. This field is booming with opportunities and is highly in demand.

The average annual salary of a UI/UX designer in the US is $101,999.

Here is a glimpse of what you will learn:

  • A deep dive into user-centered design principles
  • Tutorials on wireframing and prototyping tools (e.g Figma, Sketch)
  • Best practices for creating user-friendly interfaces
  • Case studies of successful UI/UX design projects
